- q is a random variable that determines the direction of the rabbit's escape. It is used to simulate the unpredictable behavior of the prey and to guide the artificial hawks towards unexplored regions of the search space.

- r is a random variable that determines the degree of exploration or exploitation of the artificial hawks. It is used to balance the trade-off between exploring new regions of the search space and exploiting the current best solution.
